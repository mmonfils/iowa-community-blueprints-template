# Repository Order of Operations: The Blueprint Execution Workflow

This document establishes the official step-by-step process for identifying local issues, verifying public facts, and deploying horizontal solutions across our neighborhoods.

---

## Step 1: Track a Local Area Issue
Identify a clear issue of public concern affecting our streets or rural roads. This framework applies directly to localized breakdowns within regional choices, including city rules, zoning codes, development plans, public works, neighborhood health safety, or affordable housing gaps.

## Step 2: Form the Initial Facts
Initiate the systemic tracking process using the **Three Scales Guide** (`determinants-framework.md`). Map out how broader decisions trickle down onto our immediate blocks, ensuring that every claim is anchored directly to verifiable open public records.

## Step 3: Write the Strategic Briefing
Translate your findings into an authoritative, simple summary using the **Local Situation Brief** (`situation-brief.md`). This formats your research into clear segments covering the exact Situation, Background, Assessment, and Recommendation.

## Step 4: Inject the Data Locally
Deploy your findings using the **Direct Injection Model**. Deliver your strategic briefs and action roadmaps quietly and directly to a highly curated, localized pool of three to seven local Catalysts (Megaphones, Operators, and Embedded Insiders).

## Step 5: Draft the Action and Media Tools
Once you receive neighborhood feedback, complete the relevant implementation templates:
* Use the **Local Action Roadmap** (`action-roadmap.md`) to set clear timelines and accountability phases.
* Use the **Economic Advocacy Matrix** (`economic-advocacy-template.md`) to track alternative financial impacts and corporate tax shifts.
* Use the **Front-Porch Communication Guide** (`community-guide.md`) to navigate and guide real-world living room talks and neighbor conversations.

## Step 6: Launch and Execute
Host the final anonymized tracking files on a static open server layout. Use your verified data assets and text alert loops to execute your roadmap steps and win lasting neighborhood protections.
